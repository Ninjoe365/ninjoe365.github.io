About us

Our humble beginning way back 2010 came from our photo booth business named Port A Memory.

A pioneering company providing photo booths in the country and making events more enjoyable and memorable. Then it continued to grow and bloom, establishing “PAM”. A well-known professional photo, video and photo booth company for weddings, events and debut in the Philippines.
